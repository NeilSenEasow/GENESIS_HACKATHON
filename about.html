About Us

Food Rescue: Nourishing Communities

Our Mission

At Food Rescue, our mission is to tackle food waste while alleviating hunger. We connect surplus food from various sources, such as restaurants, grocery stores, and events, directly to those in need in our community. By redistributing excess food, we aim to create a sustainable solution to food insecurity and support the well-being of vulnerable populations.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no edible food goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious meals. Through collaboration with local businesses, volunteers, and organizations, we strive to build a network that fosters compassion and responsibility, ultimately contributing to a healthier, more equitable society.

Our Impact

  • Over 100,000 meals redistributed annually
  • Partnerships with 200+ local food providers
  • Reduction of food waste by 50% in participating businesses
  • Support for 5,000+ families in need each month
